Strategy #4: The Hit List Method

Find agencies that ALREADY BUY what you sell using USAspending.gov

  • Go to USAspending.gov ‚Üí Advanced Search - Search past contract awards
  • Filter by your NAICS + Set-Aside type - Match YOUR certifications
  • Set award amount: $25K - $250K - Less competition sweet spot
  • Export results - These agencies BUY what you sell

The Key Insight

USAspending shows PAST awards. Find who's buying ‚Üí Then check SAM.gov for ACTIVE opportunities from those same agencies.

Strategy #5: Expiring Contracts

The fastest path to contracts: Position on recompetes BEFORE they drop.

Path A: Team WITH Incumbent

  • They need small business subs
  • Lower risk for you
  • Build past performance

Path B: Compete AGAINST

  • Position as better option
  • Higher risk, higher reward
  • Need strong past performance

Sweet Spot Timing

12-18 months before expiration. That's when agencies start market research.
