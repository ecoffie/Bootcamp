How to Use This Template

The STAR Method

Structure each past performance write-up using Situation, Task, Action, Result - then add a Relevance statement connecting to the current opportunity.

S
Situation

Describe the client's challenge or need you were hired to address. Set the context.

T
Task

Explain your specific responsibilities and the scope of work you were contracted to perform.

A
Action

Detail the approach and methods you used to accomplish the task. Be specific about HOW you delivered.

R
Result

Quantify the outcomes: savings, improvements, metrics achieved. Use numbers whenever possible.

Example Past Performance Write-Up

IT Infrastructure Modernization - Department of Veterans Affairs

Contract: VA123-20-C-0456 | $2.4M | June 2022 - June 2024 | Prime Contractor

Situation: The VA's legacy infrastructure was causing frequent system outages affecting veteran services at 12 regional offices. The aging systems could not support modern telehealth requirements, resulting in appointment delays and increased veteran complaints.

Task: [Company] was contracted to modernize the IT infrastructure across all 12 regional offices, including network upgrades, server virtualization, and implementation of cloud-based telehealth systems. The contract required zero downtime during business hours and completion within 18 months.

Action: We deployed our proven phased migration methodology, conducting upgrades during nights and weekends. Our team implemented redundant systems before transitions to ensure continuous operations. We utilized Agile project management with weekly stakeholder updates and maintained an on-site presence at each office during critical migration windows.

Result: Completed project 2 months ahead of schedule with zero unplanned downtime. Reduced system response time by 65%. Enabled telehealth capabilities resulting in 40% increase in virtual appointments. Decreased IT support tickets by 78%. Received "Exceptional" rating on CPARS.

Relevance: This experience directly demonstrates our capability to perform the infrastructure modernization required under this solicitation. Our proven methodology for zero-downtime migrations, combined with our experience working within VA security requirements, positions us to deliver immediate value to this program.

Past Performance Best Practices

Do's

  • Use specific numbers and metrics whenever possible
  • Choose references that are similar in scope, size, and complexity
  • Contact references BEFORE listing them
  • Include CPARS ratings if they are positive
  • Explain relevance explicitly - don't make evaluators guess

Don'ts

  • Don't use generic descriptions without specifics
  • Don't list references without confirming they will respond
  • Don't include contracts with poor performance history
  • Don't exceed page limits - be concise
  • Don't use outdated contracts when newer ones exist
