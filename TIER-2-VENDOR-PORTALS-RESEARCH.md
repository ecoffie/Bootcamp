## Research Methodology

1. **Website Search:** Visit each company's official website and look for:
   - "Suppliers" or "Vendors" section
   - "Procurement" or "Subcontracting" pages
   - "Small Business" or "Diverse Supplier" programs

2. **Common Portal Platforms:**
   - SupplierOne (used by KPMG, RTX, Dow)
   - Coupa Supplier Portal (CSP)
   - SAP Ariba Network
   - Custom company portals

3. **Contact SBLO Directly:** For smaller companies or if portal not found, email the SBLO contact to inquire about vendor registration process.
