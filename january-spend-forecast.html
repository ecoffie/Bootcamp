January Spending Dynamics

  • $

    Post-Holiday Procurement Surge

    Contracting offices return from holiday slowdown with Q1 deadlines looming. Expect increased solicitation activity as agencies play catch-up on December delays.

  • !

    Q1 Fund Obligation Pressure

    Many agencies must obligate a percentage of their annual budget by end of Q1 (December 31) but can issue modifications through January. This creates urgency for contract actions.

  • *

    New Calendar Year Contracts

    Calendar-year-based contracts often start in January. Primes are actively onboarding subcontractors for newly awarded work.

  • +

    NDAA Implementation Begins

    FY2026 NDAA programs start implementation. New initiatives in cyber, AI, and defense modernization mean new contract opportunities.
