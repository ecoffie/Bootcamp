Quick government finance lesson:

Federal agencies operate on a
"USE IT OR LOSE IT" budget

If they don't spend their allocated budget by end of fiscal year, they get LESS money next year.

RESULT?

Agencies go on a SPENDING SPREE
in Q4 (especially December)

This is called the "spend forecast" - and it's pure gold for contractors.

YOUR DECEMBER SPEND FORECAST SHOWS:

• Which agencies have money to spend

• What they're buying

• How much they need to obligate

WHY THIS MATTERS FOR SUBCONTRACTING:

Prime contractors need to meet their small business subcontracting goals too.

When agencies push contracts through fast, primes need partners FAST.

Response times go from:

"2-4 weeks" → "Can you start Monday?"

Strategy: Open your December Spend Forecast. Cross-reference with your Prime Contractor Directory. Target the primes working with agencies that have Q4 spending pressure.
