## üí° PRO TIPS FOR SUCCESS

### Before Reaching Out:
1. ‚úÖ Prepare your capability statement (1-2 pages)
2. ‚úÖ Have your NAICS codes ready
3. ‚úÖ Know your certifications (8(a), SDVOSB, WOSB, etc.)
4. ‚úÖ Research the company's contracts (USAspending.gov)
5. ‚úÖ Prepare your elevator pitch (30 seconds)

### During Outreach:
1. ‚úÖ Personalize each email (mention their company specifically)
2. ‚úÖ Keep initial email short (under 150 words)
3. ‚úÖ Be specific about your capabilities
4. ‚úÖ Ask for next steps (don't just "attach and hope")
5. ‚úÖ Track all communications in CRM

### Follow-up Best Practices:
1. ‚úÖ Wait 5-7 business days before first follow-up
2. ‚úÖ Try 2-3 times total before moving on
3. ‚úÖ Vary your contact method (email ‚Üí phone ‚Üí LinkedIn)
4. ‚úÖ Add value in each touch (new info, not "just checking in")
