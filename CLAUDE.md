# GovCon Giants - Bootcamp Project

**Last Updated:** February 4, 2026
**Owner:** Eric Coffie
**Purpose:** Federal contracting bootcamp materials, marketing assets, and business tools

---

## Project Overview

This repository contains all GovCon Giants resources for helping small businesses win federal government contracts, including:
- Monthly bootcamp materials and landing pages
- Contract opportunity hit lists
- SBLO (Small Business Liaison Officer) contact databases
- Prime contractor databases (Tier 1 & Tier 2)
- Marketing funnels and email sequences
- YouTube content and presentations
- AI/automation tools and scripts

## The 8 Agency Pain Points

1. **End-of-Year Spending Urgency** - Q4 budget spending pressure
2. **Small Business Goals & Set-Aside Requirements** - 23% goal compliance
3. **Specialized Technical Expertise Gaps** - Niche skills shortage
4. **Legacy System Modernization** - IT/cloud migration needs
5. **Construction & Facilities Management** - Infrastructure backlogs
6. **Rapid Response & Emergency Needs** - Quick turnaround requirements
7. **Supply Chain & Logistics Challenges** - Reliable supplier needs
8. **Research & Development Innovation** - SBIR/STTR programs
