Why Target Recompete Contracts?

  • Pre-Defined Requirements: The government already knows what they need - proposals are clearer
  • Incumbent Weaknesses: Current contractors often become complacent - your fresh approach wins
  • Known Budget: Funding is already allocated and often already appropriated
  • Shorter Timeline: Recompetes often move faster than new requirements
  • FY26 NDAA Advantage: New sole source thresholds ($8M/$10M) mean more small business opportunities
  • Unobligated Balance Pressure: Agencies must spend or lose - recompetes are priority
