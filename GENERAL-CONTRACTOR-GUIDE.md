## Best Opportunities for GCs in the Real List

Looking at the SAM.gov export, GCs should prioritize:

### 1. **VA Hospital Construction Projects**
- **Example:** "Zuni Roof Replacement and Fall Protection Construction"
- **NAICS:** Commercial and Institutional Building Construction
- **Set-Aside:** Total Small Business Set-Aside
- **Why:** Large projects, clear scopes, multiple similar opportunities

### 2. **Electrical System Upgrades**
- **Example:** "Upgrade Campus Electrical Systems"
- **NAICS:** 238210 (Electrical Contractors) or 236220 (Building Construction)
- **Set-Aside:** Often SDVOSB or Small Business
- **Why:** Always needed, good value, clear requirements

### 3. **HVAC and Plumbing Work**
- **Example:** "Replace Parking Garage Sprinkler System"
- **NAICS:** 238220 (Plumbing, Heating, AC Contractors)
- **Set-Aside:** SDVOSB or Small Business
- **Why:** Maintenance work, recurring opportunities

### 4. **Facility Upgrades**
- **Example:** "Upgrade Environmental Controls Building"
- **NAICS:** 236220 or 238290
- **Set-Aside:** SDVOSB or Small Business
- **Why:** Renovation work, GCs excel at coordinating multiple trades

## Action Plan for General Contractors

### Step 1: Search the Real Opportunities List
1. Open `december-hit-list-real-opportunities.html`
2. Search for keywords: "Construction", "Building", "Electrical", "Plumbing", "HVAC"
3. Look for NAICS codes: **236220**, **238210**, **238220**, **237110**, **561210**

### Step 2: Filter by Set-Aside Type
- **SDVOSB** - If you're a service-disabled veteran-owned business
- **8(a)** - If you're 8(a) certified
- **HUBZone** - If you're in a HUBZone
- **Small Business** - If you meet small business size standards

### Step 3: Check Response Deadlines
- Many construction projects have deadlines in **December 2025 - January 2026**
- Site visits are often required - plan ahead!

### Step 4: Review Requirements
- Bonding requirements (often needed for construction)
- Licensing requirements (varies by state)
- Security clearances (sometimes needed for military bases)

## Quick Wins for GCs

### 1. VA Medical Center Projects
- **Why:** VA has massive construction needs, many set-asides
- **Search:** SAM.gov for "VA" + "Construction" + your state
- **NAICS:** 236220 (Commercial Building Construction)

### 2. Military Base Renovations
- **Why:** Multiple bases, recurring maintenance needs
- **Search:** Army/Navy/Air Force + "facilities" + "construction"
- **NAICS:** 236220 or 561210

### 3. Federal Building Upgrades
- **Why:** GSA and other agencies need building improvements
- **Search:** GSA + "construction" + "renovation"
- **NAICS:** 236220
