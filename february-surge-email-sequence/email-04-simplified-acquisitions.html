If you're new to proposals, don't start with a $5 million RFP.

Start with simplified acquisitions.

What are simplified acquisitions?

Contracts under $250,000 that use streamlined procedures. Shorter responses, faster decisions, and a much better learning environment.

Here's why they're perfect for getting started:

  • Shorter responses - Often 5-15 pages vs. 50+ for full RFPs
  • Faster turnaround - 7-21 day response windows are common
  • Simpler evaluation - Often LPTA (Lowest Price Technically Acceptable)
  • Build past performance - Win small, then go bigger

At the Proposal Writing Surge, we dedicate an entire session to simplified acquisitions:

  • Quote vs. Proposal - understanding the difference
  • SF 1449 and commercial item acquisitions
  • LPTA vs. Best Value evaluation factors
  • Formatting a simplified acquisition response
