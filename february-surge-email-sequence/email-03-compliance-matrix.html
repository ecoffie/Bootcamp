Ask any professional proposal writer what they do FIRST when an RFP drops.

It's not writing the technical approach.

It's not working on pricing.

It's building the compliance matrix.

What is a compliance matrix?

A document that maps every single requirement from the RFP to your proposal response. It ensures you address everything the evaluators will score you on.

Here's why it matters:

  • Missing ONE requirement can disqualify your entire proposal
  • Evaluators check compliance BEFORE scoring quality
  • It keeps your team on the same page during writing
  • It makes your final review much faster and more accurate
