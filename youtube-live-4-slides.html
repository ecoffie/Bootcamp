The Golden Rule of Buyer Outreach

FRAME EVERYTHING AROUND THEIR GOALS - NOT YOURS
You're not asking for a favor. You're offering to solve their problem.
DON'T Say:
"I'm looking for opportunities..."
"We'd love to work with you..."
"Can you keep us in mind..."
"We need contracts to grow..."
DO Say:
"I can help you meet your SB goals..."
"We solve [specific problem]..."
"Here's how we reduce your risk..."
"We've delivered [result] for [agency]..."

Ask yourself: "What's in it for THEM?"
