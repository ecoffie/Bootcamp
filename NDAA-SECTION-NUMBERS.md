## Small Business Provisions (2026 NDAA)

### Known Provisions:
1. **Unified Application Process** - DoD must establish single application process
2. **Increased Sole Source Thresholds:**
   - Non-manufacturing: $8M (up from $4.5M)
   - Manufacturing: $10M (up from $7M)
3. **Enhanced Subcontracting Transparency** - Primes must provide utilization plans
4. **Cybersecurity Harmonization** - Deadline: June 1, 2026
5. **CMMC Support Strategy** - Deadline: January 31, 2026
6. **Supply Chain Transparency** - Database deadline: January 1, 2027
7. **Nontraditional Contractor Exemptions** - FAR Part 31 and TINA exemptions

## How to Find Section Numbers

1. **Go to Full Text:** Click "Text" tab on Congress.gov
2. **Search:** Use Ctrl+F (Cmd+F on Mac) to search for:
   - Agency names (e.g., "Army", "Navy", "Air Force")
   - Program names (e.g., "Procurement", "RDT&E", "Shipbuilding")
   - Small business keywords (e.g., "small business", "set-aside", "sole source")
3. **Note Format:** Sections are numbered (e.g., "Sec. 9083" or "Section 9083")
4. **Check Table of Contents:** May have organized list of sections
