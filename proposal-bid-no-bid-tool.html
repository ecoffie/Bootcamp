How to Use This Tool

Scoring Instructions

Rate each criterion from 0-2 based on how well it applies to your opportunity:

0 = No/Poor - Does not meet criterion
1 = Partial - Partially meets criterion
2 = Yes/Strong - Fully meets criterion

Scoring Criteria

1. Technical Capability (Weight: High)

Core Competency Match
Does this work align with your core capabilities?
Technical Requirements
Can you meet ALL technical requirements without significant gaps?
Key Personnel
Do you have or can you quickly recruit required key personnel?

2. Past Performance (Weight: High)

Relevant Experience
Do you have 2-3 similar contracts you can reference?
Similar Scope & Size
Is your past performance similar in scope and dollar value?
Agency Experience
Have you worked with this agency or similar agencies before?

3. Competitive Position (Weight: Medium)

Incumbent Status
Are you the incumbent or do you have relationship with evaluators?
Competitive Landscape
Is this a fair competition or is there a likely preferred vendor?
Certifications/Set-Aside
Do you have the required certifications for set-asides?

4. Strategic Fit (Weight: Medium)

Strategic Value
Does this align with your business development strategy?
Profitability
Can you achieve acceptable margins at competitive pricing?
Growth Potential
Does this open doors for future opportunities?

5. Proposal Resources (Weight: Medium)

Response Time
Do you have enough time to prepare a quality proposal?
Proposal Team
Do you have the team/resources to write a competitive proposal?
Opportunity Cost
Will pursuing this prevent you from better opportunities?
